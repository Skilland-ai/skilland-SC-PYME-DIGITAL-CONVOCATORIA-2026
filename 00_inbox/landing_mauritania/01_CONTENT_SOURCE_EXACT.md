# Landing Skilland — Sistema de Transferencia de Conocimiento para Proyectos Europeos

## 1. Idea central

La landing no debe vender “una plataforma”. Debe vender una **categoría nueva**:

> **Sistema de Transferencia de Conocimiento para proyectos europeos**

El gran giro estratégico es este:

> Los proyectos europeos generan conocimiento, metodologías, aprendizajes, casos, expertos y resultados.  
> El problema es que demasiado de eso se queda en entregables, PDFs, jornadas y reporting.  
> **Skilland lo convierte en formación viva, reutilizable, medible y escalable.**

Ese es el corazón de la narrativa.

El deck ya apunta a esa dirección:

```text
Conocimiento experto
→ Motor de transferencia
→ Formación práctica
→ Empresas, emprendedores, profesionales y tejido productivo
```

## 12. FAQ / objections

### Is this just an LMS?

No. An LMS hosts learning content. Skilland helps transform expert knowledge and project outputs into structured, reusable and measurable training experiences.

### We already have Moodle / a training platform. Why would we need this?

Because the bottleneck is often not hosting content. The bottleneck is turning fragmented knowledge, expert insight and project outputs into high-quality learning assets that people can actually use.

### Our experts do not have time to create courses.

That is exactly the point. Skilland reduces the friction for experts by helping capture, structure and transform their knowledge into training without asking them to become instructional designers.

### Can each partner manage its own content?

Yes. The system can support a shared project environment while allowing different partners or entities to manage their own training spaces, experts and audiences.

### Can it support dissemination and reporting?

Yes. Training participation, progress, completion and feedback can become useful evidence for communication, dissemination, exploitation and impact reporting.

### Is this only for formal training?

No. It can support courses, capsules, pathways, onboarding materials, practical guides, sector resources, expert knowledge libraries and capacity-building programmes.

### Can content be reused after the project ends?

That is one of the main advantages. The goal is to turn project knowledge into reusable assets that remain useful beyond isolated events or deliverables.
