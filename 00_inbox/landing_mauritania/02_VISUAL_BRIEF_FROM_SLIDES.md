# Visual brief from AfricanTech / Skilland slides

This file replaces the PDF as visual input. The landing builder must recreate these ideas as HTML/CSS visual sections or diagrams. Do not require the original PDF.

## Visual 1 — Knowledge transfer engine flow

Use this visual idea in the hero or early system section.

Structure:

Left side:
- Institutions
- Experts
- Companies

All three flow into a central glowing pill/card:

- Skilland Knowledge Transfer Engine
- Practical training resources

From the central engine, flow out to:

- Companies
- Entrepreneurs
- Professionals
- Productive sector

Message:

AfricanTech / Skilland helps turn distributed expertise into accessible, practical and measurable training for the productive sector.

Design direction:

- horizontal flow
- soft teal/mint glow around the central engine
- simple icons for institutions, experts, companies and beneficiaries
- clean white background
- premium SaaS / institutional innovation feel
- avoid copying the slide literally; recreate the concept as a landing diagram

## Visual 2 — Four-step knowledge transformation process

Use this visual idea in the “How knowledge transfer works” section.

Process:

1. Capture expert knowledge
2. Structure it into learning content
3. Publish courses, capsules and pathways
4. Track progress, participation and impact

Supporting orbit / labels:

- Asynchronous learning
- Personalised content generation
- AI tutor
- Progress tracking
- Expert support

Message:

Skilland reduces the friction between knowing and teaching. Experts do not need to become instructional designers to transfer their knowledge.

Design direction:

- horizontal process cards or connected chevrons
- each step with icon + short label
- circular/orbit effect optional
- teal/navy/mint palette
- clear enough to understand in 5 seconds
